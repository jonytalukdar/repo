About Us

This briefing memorandum, contains confidential information about OLYMPIC REFINERY AND PETROCHEMICAL RESOURCES LTD (OLYMPIC GROUP) the company which is intended primarily for the intention of giving information to a selected list of companies and institutions to whom it is addressed to, for evaluating potential financing opportunities and projects.

INCORPORATION

OLYMPIC REFINERY AND PETROCHEMICAL RESOURCES LTD (RC NO: 1096814) was incorporated in Nigeria in February 2013 under the companies and allied matters act 1990 with irdubart share capital of Ten Million Naira (N10,000,000) divided into Ten Million (10,000,000) of one Naira (N1.00) each, however Olympic Refinery raised her share capital to fifty million by additional forty million shares in february, 2014.

MISSION

Olympic Refinery and Petrochemical Resources Ltd, which is a special purpose vehicle designed to bring development to communities, enhance their living standards and conditions.

We will search for oil, gas and condensate in the most efficient, economic and environmentally friendly manner, and will subsequently produce, transport, manage and deliver these products. Olympic aims at securing high sustainable return on investment at the same time, Olympic will properly take care of the interest of all parties involved including but not limited to the following shareholders, investors, suppliers, public authorities, employees and third parties.

VISION

Our purpose is to engage in energy and energy related fields, acquiring OPLs and engaging in exploration and production activities for petroleum products in shallow marine and offshore deep in Nigeria under production sharing contracts with viable end users and technical partners and the Nigerian National petroleum Corporation (NNPC), as the contractor. We plan to create a consortium of highly trained and experienced professional staff that will engage in exploration, development and production of hydrocarbons. We plan to be an integral part of Nigerian economic development, creating jobs which will be of direct benefit to the people of Nigeria.

SCOPE OF ACTIVITIES
  • Oil Exploration and Production

  • Petrochemicals development
  • Gas development and utilization
  • Oil and Gas research and development
  • Oil and Gas Engineering

  • Marketing of Nigeria's accruable crude.

Our objectives include but are not limited to conducting exploration and production, getting involved in both down and upstream activities. We will harvest marginal fields and develop some with new technologies. Our overall plans must tap into establishing pipelines/Gas Company, and manage pipeline networks across Nigeria. We will enter and establish agreements with foreign refiners and marketers, besides striving to own ours in Nigeria.

Olympic will establish partnership agreements with both majors and minors for mutual benefits. We plan to own and establish tank farms in Nigeria. We will train our staff and possibly enter into mutual agreements with minority participation in oil producing States. Our social responsibility will include providing social amenities to the Local communities fostering safety in the environment and among employees.

To search for, inspect, examine, explore, work, take on lease, purchase, or otherwise acquire lands and places which may seem to the Company capable or possibly capable of affording supply of mineral oil and to establish, utilize and turn to account pumping stations. Pipelines and other works and conveniences suitable for the purpose. To carry on and run industrial projects of all kinds and in particular, those involving products and equipment related to petroleum and petrochemical industry. Other areas of interest include but not Limited to: we plan to take advantage of the incentives provided for by the Federal Government of Nigeria.
